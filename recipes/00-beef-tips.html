ALDENTE AGHAST

He's back and ready to share with you the latest sensation from New Hill, NC. This time - beef tips tartare.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of organic beef tips
  • Lettuce
  • 3 cups of kosher sea salt
  • 5 cups of Mediterranean couscous

Preparation: Steps

  1. Season your steak using the steak marinade in existence. Let it set for 24 hours before prep.
  2. Grill for 15 minutes on medium-high heat.
  3. Add some garnishes.
  4. Take pic
